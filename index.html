URGENT ASSISTANCE REQUIRED!

Little Plutina is lost in space. She fell asleep at the controls of her Z Force Space Hopper Deluxe and has found herself far, far away from her home, the dwarf planet of Pluto. Her spaceship has made its way to Mercury, right at the other end of the solar system. It runs on the fuel of knowledge but unfortunately the fuel tank is nearly dry. To refuel she needs to provide the answers to some questions but she's feeling a bit confused after her voyage through space. Can come to Plutina's rescue and help her to make her way home?

Rules


  1. You need to travel to each of the 8 planets in the solar system to reach Pluto
  2. You start your journey with one block of fuel in the tank
  3. On each planet you need to answer a question related to your location.
  4. If you guess the answer correctly the first time, you get enough fuel to move on plus a bonus block of fuel
  5. If your second guess is correct you receive just enough fuel to travel the next planet.
  6. If you get the answer wrong on your second try you lose one block of fuel and need to answer the next question to move on
  7. If your fuel is at 0 and your second guess is wrong you move back to the previous planet
  8. Your mission is to help Plutina reach Pluto with as much remaining fuel as possible.
